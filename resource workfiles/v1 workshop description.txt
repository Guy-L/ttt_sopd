[i]A sword with your name on it. What a triumph is that...[/i]

[b]Sword of <Player> Defeat[/b] is an item for TTT2 which targets a single opponent chosen randomly each round, takes on their name (before and after buying), and allows you to loudly kill them and them alone. It's pretty much a loud knife that can only kill a single person (if they aren't already dead), but it at least prevents the target from damaging you, shows you where they are, and a couple other nifty things. 

Inspired by [i][url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0Dpw0VvH4m0]"1, 2, Oatmeal"[/url][/i] and made for the YoWaddup TTT servers - probably makes no sense at all if you haven't heard the song. Special thanks to JoelyMoly, Spanospy, dinke, Renteks, Snuffles and really everybody over at YoWaddup. And big ups to [url=https://github.com/WilliamVenner/gmpublisher]gmpublisher[/url]!

[h2]Mechanics[/h2]
([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img] = can be configured by admin; see below table)
[list]
[*][b]Target selection[/b]: A single target is randomly selected at the start of each round out of everyone that isn't team Traitor, Jackal or Infected (yes, this includes Jesters [img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img] and other side teams). Their name is inserted into the item's inventory and shop name, e.g. [i]"Sword of dinke Defeat"[/i]. If no target can be selected, if the target would be the only other player, or if the chosen target disconnects mid-round, the Sword will have no target and can be used against anyone (with some target-specific effects removed).
[*][b]Target defeat[/b]: While you hold the Sword, it'll make noise and show you an outline of where the target is ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]). By default, the target cannot deal damage to you while you're holding it ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]). Killing the target will also make a noise that can be heard from far away ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]), and the Sword itself will stop making noise when the target dies for any reason. If you have the Sword but its intended target is dead, you can at least stab their corpse with it (destroying evidence of their actual death cause in the process [img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]). If you have the Sword and you [i]are[/i] its target, you can defeat yourself with it. The Sword can reach 50% farther than the knife ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]), leaves no DNA ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]), and you get a speed boost while holding it ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]).
[*][b]Stealth factor[/b]: The more opponents are still alive, the more the Sword's relatively loud noises will be reduced. By default, at 10 opponents alive or more ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]), sound effect volume is reduced by 50% ([img]https://i.imgur.com/kPuL47k.png[/img]). This reduction goes away with less and less opponents alive, and stops happening when only one - the target - remains (if this rare case comes up, another special thing will happen...). [i]Note: "Opponents" are anyone that could be selected as the target of the Sword, minus Jesters.[/i]
[*][spoiler]The Sword reacts in a minor cosmetic way if the target player is "King Dedede".[/spoiler] 
[*][b]Pack-a-Punch[/b]: It can be packed!.. I could go into details but I think you'd rather find out by playing :)  [spoiler]Kirby inhaled his enemies and makes it la-a-a-ater...[/spoiler] 
[/list]

[b]Concept & Balance[/b]: The original idea for this item was a total [url=https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=BM]bm[/url] version of the knife that's much harder to use but could lead to funny moments, especially late-game. Since it's so comically dangerous for the T to use, it wouldn't really feel bad to get killed by since "why wouldn't you just buy a knife". I've followed through with that in its design, but since then added many convars that you could tweak to actually make it an acceptable if highly situational side-grade to the knife (see below list). Credit price is TBD - maybe less than knife to mitigate how bad it is, the same if it's considered a side-grade, or more to make it sting even harder.

[b]Intended Server Setup[/b]: This weapon was only tested for TTT2 and may not work for base TTT. It was made with the role selection of YoWaddup TTT in mind, and may not work for/against some roles. It's meant to be available in the Traitor team and Jackal team shops, and can work in a deathmatch context (the sword will be targetless). It might make more sense to make it only buyable once per team, but the idea of multiple Ts running at someone with this is funny, so up to you.
